Fighting Fires and Thanking God

Deploying with the National Guard to the Okanogan Fire Complex

It's hard to describe the emotional highs and lows of being deployed to your home state to battle the largest wildfire in Washington's history. On one hand, I was excited to be a part of the response to this devastating disaster. On the other, my heart was broken for the residents of Okanogan county and the three fire fighters who had already lost their lives in Twisp. This short pictorial is my attempt to tell a little bit of the story of my unit's deployment to Okanogan County in August and September of 2015.
